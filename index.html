Cocktails, mocktails, smoothies, milkshakes: drink it all with drinKit.

drinKit is a tutorial application which will help you make your favourite drink recipes from scratch while learning new drink making skills.

What you want to drink, right there.

Type in an ingredient or the name of a drink you fancy to search all of drinKit’s offered recipes based on what you want. Narrow down your search even further using our filtering options, such as type of drink or something you want to avoid. Especially useful for those with dietary requirements.

Love a recipe? Tell your friends.

Post what you’ve been making to your favourite social media sites from its recipe page. If you particularly liked a drink and want to bring it to people’s attention, this is a great way to let them know.

Keep drinks you liked in one place for next time.

Each recipe page offers a button to allow you to add it to your Favourites. This puts the recipe in a convenient place in your side menu, so you can quickly find that smoothie you’ve been craving or the cocktail you saved to make for that party.
